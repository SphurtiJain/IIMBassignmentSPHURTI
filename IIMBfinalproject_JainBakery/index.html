Jain Bakery

HOMEMADE sweetness. Filled with LOVE...

Nestled in the heart of our neighborhood in Vasundhara, Ghaziabad... Jain Bakery is more than just a home-based bakery... it's a labor of love born from a passion for baking and a desire to bring joy to every table.
Founded by Meenu Jain, who believes that the warmth of a home can be shared through the aroma of freshly baked goods, our bakery has become a trusted name for quality and heartfelt service.
